Hobbies & Interests

Things that bring me joy.

A surprise trip to Norway from my children sparked the excitement of traveling within me! I discovered that I love to try new foods, to experience new cultures, meet new people and be a part of their beautiful countries! I have since visited Scotland, Ireland, Spain, Europe, Sweden and London. Each country bringing newfound love for a new culture. If I have my way, many more trips are in my future!

Warm, sunny days you will find me riding my bike. It's not about how far or how fast, but how much time I get to spend thinking, enjoying the sunshine on my face and being in the outdoors away from it all

Skiing and snowboarding became a family sport when my children were young. We have enjoyed many hours on the slopes together over the years. Our annual family ski trip will continue to be one of the grandest gifts for me. Watching (and helping) my children teach their children how to ski or snowboard brings me so much joy! Being a Mom and Grandma is truly one of the greatest parts of me...

I have always been a sucker for a good book! Once the kids are in bed and I get some quiet time, reading a really good book is usually where you can find me. I really hope I have enstilled the love for reading into my own children. Our book collection is pretty amazing!

One of the things about me that brings me more happiness than my own children, is the love I have for my grandchildren. Being a Grandma is truly one of the greatest pleasures of life! Babysitting, sleepovers, birthday lunches and shopping, hours and hours at the park swinging and sliding, staying up late watching movies, making cookies together, being their biggest cheerleader at piano recitals and dance concerts, playing games and being silly...this is what makes it all worth it!
